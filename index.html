Got Questions?

Capush is a mobile app that helps you save content and references into visual collections called cards. It is built for the good things you find online and want to come back to later.

A card is a themed collection. It can be about travel, restaurants, workouts, skincare, recipes, articles, gift ideas, or anything else. Each saved item appears with useful details like image, title, description, and source when available.

Capush is broader and more practical. It works with more content types, more sources, and more everyday use cases. It is built to help you save, organize, and retrieve what matters, not just collect images.

Open Capush, tap the plus button, create a new card, and start adding links. You can also save directly from Safari or other apps using the share sheet.

Yes. You can keep cards private or share them with friends, followers, or anyone else. Capush works well as a personal tool and as a way to share curated collections.

You can save links, notes, media, and references from apps and websites like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Google Maps, Reddit, Substack, Spotify, and more.

Create separate cards for different topics, reorder items inside a card, and use search to quickly find what you saved later.
